Vinyl is lighter on budget and maintenance; fiber cement takes paint, reads as premium, and resists impact better. We quote both so you compare real numbers.

Can insurance cover siding?

If it's storm damage, often yes — siding is a standard claim line item. We document all elevations during the free inspection.
