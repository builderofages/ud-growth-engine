Why Metal

What metal buys you over asphalt

2-3x the lifespan

Asphalt runs 15-25 years in the DMV's freeze-thaw cycles. Properly installed standing seam runs 40-70. You buy one roof instead of three.

Storm resilience

Common Questions

Straight answers

Is a metal roof louder in rain?

No — over solid decking and underlayment, a metal roof is no louder than asphalt. The rain-on-a-barn sound comes from open-frame structures.

Can you install metal over my existing shingles?

Sometimes, but we usually recommend tear-off so decking issues get found and fixed — that's what protects the 50-year lifespan.

Does metal cost more?

Upfront, yes — typically 2-3x asphalt. Over the roof's life it's usually cheaper per year, and it adds resale value.
