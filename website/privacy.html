Information we collect

When you request an inspection, quote, referral reward, or job application, we collect what you give us: name, phone number, email, property address, and details about your project or claim. When you use our live chat or speak with our AI assistants, the conversation content is processed to answer your questions and to brief the human team member who follows up. Our satellite quote tool processes the address you enter to estimate roof dimensions. We do not collect payment card information through this website, and we never ask for it by phone or chat.

AI assistants and call recording

Our website chat and some phone lines are answered by AI assistants, working alongside our human team. Conversations with these assistants may be recorded, transcribed, and reviewed to schedule your appointment, document your request, and improve service quality. By continuing a chat or call after this disclosure, you consent to that processing. You can always ask for a human, and a team member will call you back.

How we use your information

To schedule and perform inspections and projects, document insurance claims at your direction, send appointment confirmations and follow-ups, process referral rewards, evaluate job applications, and operate and improve this website. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Calls and texts (TCPA consent)

When you submit a form with your phone number, you agree that United Developers may call or text you about your request, including through automated systems and AI assistants. Consent is not a condition of purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to any text to opt out, or tell any assistant or team member to remove you from contact.

Service providers

We use a small number of vendors to operate this site: form delivery, conversational AI processing for chat and voice, and mapping/satellite imagery services. These providers process your information only to provide their service to us. We do not use advertising trackers on this site.

Retention and your choices

We keep project and claim records as long as needed for warranties, licensing, and legal requirements. You may request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete what we are not legally required to keep — email office@udroofing.com and we will respond within 30 days.

Children

This site is for homeowners and job applicants 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
