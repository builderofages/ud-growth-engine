Quick questions

Does insurance cover storm roof damage?

If a covered storm (hail or wind) caused the damage and your claim is approved, insurance typically pays for the repair or replacement — and your out-of-pocket is usually just your deductible. We document the damage and meet the adjuster on-site.

How do I know if I even have damage?

Hail bruising and granule loss are usually invisible from the ground. That's exactly why the free inspection matters — we document every slope with dated photos so you know for sure.

Is the inspection really free?

Yes — free, no obligation, no pressure. If there's no damage, we'll tell you honestly.

How fast can you come out?
