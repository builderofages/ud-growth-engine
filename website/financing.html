How It Works

Three ways DMV homeowners pay for a roof

Insurance pays — you pay the deductible

Storm damage? On approved claims, insurance covers the roof and your out-of-pocket is typically just the deductible — usually $1,000–$2,500. We document, file, and meet the adjuster with you.

MOST POPULAR

$0 down monthly financing

No storm? No problem. Finance the full project with $0 down on approved credit — terms from 12 to 180 months. Even your deductible can be financed, turning a surprise bill into a small monthly payment.

Pay as you go

Prefer cash or card? Standard schedule on insurance work is half at the first insurance check, half at the final — never large sums up front. That's how a licensed contractor is supposed to operate.
