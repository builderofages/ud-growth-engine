Roofing 101

Materials at a glance.

Architectural shingles

Most popular. Dimensional asphalt, ~25–30 yr, GAF Timberline HDZ flagship.

3-tab shingles

Budget, flat, ~15–20 yr. Mostly phased out.

Standing-seam metal

Premium. 40–70 yr, concealed fasteners, energy efficient.

TPO / EPDM (flat)

Commercial/low-slope membranes. TPO = white welded; EPDM = black rubber.

📏 A “square” = 100 sq ft. Pitch = rise/run (e.g. 6/12). Most leaks come from flashing, not field shingles.

Reading Damage

Hail vs wind — know the signs.

Hail

  • · Random bruising / soft spots
  • · Granule loss exposing black asphalt
  • · Dented gutters, vents, AC fins (collateral)
  • · Often invisible from the ground

Wind

  • · Creased / lifted / curled shingles
  • · Missing shingles, exposed nails
  • · Broken seal strip (even if it looks intact)
Insurance Essentials

Speak the adjuster's language.

  • ✓ ACV = replacement cost − depreciation · RCV = full replacement (depreciation paid back after the work)
  • ✓ Deductible = the homeowner's only cost. It's owed by law — never offer to "waive" it (fraud)
  • ✓ Supplement = get paid for missed items: code upgrades, O&P, extra layers
  • ✓ Xactimate is the pricing software — knowing it builds adjuster credibility
  • ✓ File by the date of loss; document the test square hail hits
