# United Developers — Demand Generation & Social System
### The marketing engine that feeds the sales floor

This is not "post more on Instagram." This is a multi-channel demand engine engineered around how DMV homeowners actually buy roofs: **storms, search, and social proof.** Every channel feeds one place — the **AI Receptionist (Agent 1)** — which books inspections. We measure everything in *booked inspections* and *cost per booked job*, not vanity metrics.

---

## 0. The strategy in one line
**Own the storm.** When hail or wind hits a Montgomery County / NoVA ZIP, UD is the first name homeowners see on Google, in their feed, in their mailbox, and at their door — within 48 hours — all routing to an AI that books the inspection instantly.

Three demand types, three plays:
| Demand type | Buyer mindset | Our play |
|---|---|---|
| **Storm-triggered** (highest ROI) | "My neighbor's getting a new roof — do I have damage?" | Rapid geo-targeted ads + door + mailers in the hit ZIPs |
| **Active search** | "roof repair near me" | Google LSA + Search + airtight local SEO |
| **Latent / brand** | Not in-market yet | Social proof content so we're the obvious choice when they are |

## 1. Channel 1 — Google (capture active demand)

**a) Google Local Services Ads (LSA) — "Google Guaranteed"**
- Highest-intent, pay-per-lead, badge builds trust. Get the Google Guaranteed badge (license + insurance + background check).
- Target: roofing, roof repair, storm damage — Montgomery County + NoVA.
- Starting budget: **$3–5K/mo**. Expect $25–75 per lead in this market; AI receptionist answers them all (LSA penalizes missed calls — this is a direct edge).

**b) Google Search Ads**
- Tight campaigns: `Storm/Hail` · `Roof Replacement` · `Roof Repair/Leak` · `Siding/Gutters`.
- Exact + phrase match. Negative keywords: "diy", "jobs", "salary", "free", "how to".
- Geo: 12-mi radius around Silver Spring + Alexandria offices; bid up on freshly-stormed ZIPs.
- Landing page: the new site's storm funnel (already built). Budget: **$4–6K/mo** scaling with close data.

**c) Google Business Profile (GBP) — the free goldmine**
- Two optimized profiles (MD + VA). Weekly photo posts of real jobs. Service + area lists complete.
- **Review engine** (see §5) to push toward hundreds of reviews → ranks in the Map Pack → free leads forever.

**Sample Search ad copy:**
```
Headline 1: Storm Damage Roof? Free Inspection
Headline 2: We Handle Your Insurance Claim
Headline 3: Most Homeowners Pay Deductible Only
Desc 1: GAF-certified DMV roofers. Free 24-hr inspection + photo report. A+ BBB, 131+ 5★.
Desc 2: Hail & wind specialists in Montgomery County & NoVA. Roof done in a day. Call now.
```

## 2. Channel 2 — Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — manufacture demand

Homeowners aren't searching until they're worried. Meta *creates* the worry-then-relief loop with proof.

**Always-on campaigns:**
- **Storm Response (geo-burst):** when a ZIP gets hit, launch a 5–15 mi radius campaign within 48 hrs: "⛈️ [City] homeowners — hail came through [date]. Free roof inspection this week. We handle the insurance." → Lead form / site funnel.
- **Proof & Authority (evergreen):** before/after reels, 1-day install time-lapses, adjuster-meeting explainers, homeowner testimonials. Objective: leads + retargeting pool.
- **Retargeting:** anyone who hit the site or watched 50%+ of a video → "Still thinking about that roof? Free inspection, no obligation."

**Creative testing discipline (this is where agencies win):** run batches of distinct creatives (hook variations: storm fear, money/deductible, speed, trust). Kill losers fast, scale the 1–2 winners. Hook rate and CTR decide, not opinions. Budget: **$3–5K/mo**, weighted toward storm bursts.

## 3. Channel 3 — Storm Response Automation (the unfair advantage)

Wire a **weather/hail data trigger** (e.g., HailTrace / Interactive Hail Maps / NOAA storm events) → when a qualifying hail/wind event hits a target ZIP:
1. Auto-launch the Meta geo-burst + Google bid boost for that ZIP.
2. Agent 2 texts past leads/customers in the ZIP ("storm just hit your area…").
3. Generate a canvasser/door route map for reps for that neighborhood.
4. Spin up a ZIP-specific landing variant ("Roof damage in [City]? We're already in your neighborhood").

Speed is the moat. Most competitors take 1–2 weeks to react; UD reacts in 48 hours, fully staffed by AI.

## 4. Channel 4 — Local SEO (compounding free leads)

- **Service-area pages:** one page per major city/ZIP — Gaithersburg 20877, Silver Spring, Wheaton, Bethesda, Rockville, Germantown, Alexandria, Arlington, Fairfax. Each: local copy, real job photos, reviews, schema markup.
- **Target keywords:** "roofing [city]", "storm damage roof repair [city]", "roof replacement [city] MD/VA", "hail damage roofing Montgomery County".
- **Technical:** fast mobile site (built), `LocalBusiness` + `Review` schema, NAP consistency across directories, embedded GBP map.
- **Content hub:** "Does insurance cover roof damage in Maryland?", "ACV vs RCV explained", "Signs of hail damage on your roof" — these rank and pre-sell the insurance story.

## 5. Review & Reputation Engine (trust = conversion)

131 reviews is good; **400+** makes UD the default choice and dominates the Map Pack.
- **Automated ask:** after every completed job, Agent 2 texts/emails a 1-tap Google review link. Time it to the "wow" moment (clean-up done, roof reveal).
- Route unhappy customers to a private service channel first (protect the public rating, fix the issue).
- Reply to **every** review (AI-drafted, human-approved) — signals an active, caring business to Google and buyers.
- Showcase fresh reviews on the site + social weekly.

**Current state (from research):** IG @uniteddevelopers_mdva with some project reels + partnerships, basic FB page, minimal X. Inconsistent and under-leveraged for a $10M company.

**New positioning:** "The DMV's storm-roof experts who make insurance easy." Educational + proof + local, posted consistently.

**Content pillars (rotate):**
1. **Proof** — before/after, 1-day install time-lapses, drone roof shots.

**Cadence:** 1 Reel/short-form video + 3–4 feed posts per week per platform (IG, FB, + start TikTok + YouTube Shorts for reach). Repurpose one shoot into 5–7 assets.
