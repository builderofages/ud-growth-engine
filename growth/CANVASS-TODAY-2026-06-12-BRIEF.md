## Legal guardrails (unchanged, non-negotiable)
- Never pay/waive/rebate a deductible (MD & VA law). Script: "insurance covers approved work; you pay your deductible."
- Document damage; never interpret policy or negotiate claim value (that's unlicensed public adjusting).
- Solicitor permits: PW, Fairfax, Stafford, PG counties generally require door-to-door permits — verify before knocking. Respect every No Soliciting sign.
- 3-day right of cancellation in any door-signed contract. No cold calls/texts (TCPA).

- In the PG County zone, someone died in this storm. Lead with service, not fear. If a home has serious damage, help first — tarp referrals, photos — the contract follows.
