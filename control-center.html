Launch in a few switches — all in one file

Everything above flips on by editing one file: website/config.js. Nothing else needs touching.

  1. Lead inbox: set LEAD_EMAIL to the company inbox, and LEAD_CC to the connected inbox so the Notion pipeline keeps filling.
  2. Analytics: paste GA4_ID, META_PIXEL_ID, and GOOGLE_ADS_ID — they self-install.
  3. SMS: drop a texting number's webhook into SMS_WEBHOOK for instant text-backs.
  4. Maps upgrade: add GOOGLE_MAPS_KEY to upgrade the satellite tool to Google imagery + Solar data.
  5. Financing: paste the lender link into LENDER_URL — every finance button points to it.
  6. Domain: point udroofing.com DNS at the repo (steps in the Handover Guide).
